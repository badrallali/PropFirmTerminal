Features

ROI Dashboard
See your real P&L — challenge fees vs. payouts — per firm and overall. Know exactly what you've earned and what it cost.

Firm Intelligence
Ranked leaderboard of every firm you've traded with. Instantly identify which firms are profitable and which drain your fees.
